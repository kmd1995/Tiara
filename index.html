About.

TiaraScoops™

Hi! I am Tiara Scoops, a 22-year-old fitness influencer currently attending Clayton State University. As I finish up my final semester, I’ll be ready to receive my Health and Fitness Management degree in the spring of 2019. I always found that my strength and confidence came from participating in sports. A competitive swimmer for ten years and a cheerleader for 4 years. My fitness journey began when I quit my cheer team because I had been bullied by my teammates. This was when I began to feel like I didn’t fit in. Falling into a deep depression and losing my identity was hard. Until one day, I decided I wasn’t going to allow negative people to take away my joy, so I stepped into the gym for the first time in years. Completely exhausted in the first three minutes and could barely complete twenty crunches, but I felt something so powerful in this moment that had changed me. From that day forward, I told myself I will go to the gym every day for 45 minutes and give it everything I have. After a couple of months, I noticed my tummy flatten and my scoops became round. I loved it. I loved myself and I believe I have mastered a technique that keeps your curves looking desirable by increasing muscle growth in your legs and glutes. Fitness should be fun and motivating. Start your journey to your dream body with my uniquely designed workouts!
